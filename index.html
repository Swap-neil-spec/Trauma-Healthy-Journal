Welcome to Our Journal

We provide awareness, solutions, and support to help people cope with trauma. Our aim is to help individuals understand their mental health and provide resources to help them on their journey to recovery.

Featured Books

How to Cope with Anxiety & Trauma

Here are some popular books to help you cope with anxiety and trauma:

Mindfulness and meditation "The Mindful Way through Anxiety: Break Free from Chronic Worry and Reclaim Your Life" by Susan M. Orsillo and Lizabeth Roemer

"Healing Trauma: A Pioneering Program for Restoring the Wisdom of Your Body" by Peter A. Levine - an informative book that emphasizes the importance of body-centered healing and offers exercises to help individuals release trauma from their bodies.

"The Courage to Heal: A Guide for Women Survivors of Child Sexual Abuse" by Ellen Bass and Laura Davis - a classic book that provides guidance and support for survivors of sexual abuse and helps individuals understand the impact of trauma on their lives.

"Trauma Stewardship: An Everyday Guide to Caring for Self While Caring for Others" by Laura van Dernoot Lipsky - a unique book that provides tools and strategies for professionals who work with trauma survivors, helping them care for themselves while caring for others.

Success Stories

John's Story

John had been struggling with depression for several years, and it was affecting every aspect of his life. With the help of a therapist and support from his friends and family, John was able to overcome his depression and improve his mental health. Today, he is happier and more fulfilled than ever before.

Samantha's Story

Samantha was diagnosed with PTSD after experiencing a traumatic event. With the help of a support group and counseling, she was able to work through her trauma and develop healthy coping mechanisms. Samantha now shares her story to help others who may be struggling with PTSD.
